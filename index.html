About

Industrial designer and product developer working at the intersection of design, engineering and craft. With a background in boatbuilding and carpentry, studied at UFG Linz, ECAL Lausanne and HKDI Hong Kong.

I guide products holistically – from initial idea through construction and prototyping to production readiness. Combining design ambition with a deep understanding of materials, manufacturing and digital tools.

Interdisciplinary, pragmatic and solution-oriented – whether collaborating with companies, engineers or working hands-on at the prototype in the workshop.
