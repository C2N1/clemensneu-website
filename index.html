B — ABOUT

Generalist by nature, designing and building across physical and digital.

— PROFILE

Fifteen years between the workbench and the CAD screen. Trained as a chef and boatbuilder, studied product design in Linz and at ECAL.

My experience spans renowned international design studios and a leadership role in a manufacturing company — overseeing planning and product development from sketch to series.

Work sits at the intersection of design, engineering and craft: products guided from initial idea through construction and prototyping to production readiness, combining design ambition with a deep understanding of materials, manufacturing and digital tools.

Whether collaborating with engineers or working hands-on at the prototype in the workshop — a pragmatic, interdisciplinary mindset, always curious to explore and evolve.
